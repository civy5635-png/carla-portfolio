My Journey Into Web Design and Digital Business

After 30 years of working retail and living paycheck to paycheck, I retired due to disability in 2023 and realized I needed to reinvent myself and start learning new skills.

With little computer experience beyond online shopping and taxes, I began teaching myself web design, digital marketing, automation, and online business tools from scratch in order to build a sustainable future working from home. What started as a necessity quickly became a passion for problem-solving, design, and helping businesses improve their digital presence. This led me to starting my own business, Ella Digital Consulting.
