My Journey Into Web Design and Digital Business

After 30 years of working retail and living paycheck to paycheck, I retired due to disability in 2023 and realized I needed to reinvent myself.

With little computer experience beyond online shopping and taxes, I began teaching myself new digital skills from scratch in order to build a future working from home.

In Defense of AI

In the process of learning, I discovered ChatGPT. Although I can't deny all the bad things about AI-the environmental damage, the blatant theft of artists and writers in order to train it-AI has done an enormous amount of good in my life. It's a place where I can ask the dumbest of questions, things that I'm sure everyone else in the world knows, and break technical things down in a way I can understand them. Over the last two years, I've figured out that I can solve almost any technical issue with ChatGPT and screenshots. I've learned how to do things that I'd have thought beyond me and discovered that I'm never too old to learn new things. In fact, learning new things keeps your mind working and alive.

My goal is to build my own business by creating websites and automation software for small businesses. Every new thing I learn gets me a little closer to that goal. Those black screens with code no longer scare me.
